ABOUT THE HACKATHON

CODE OLYMPICS 2025 IS A PREMIER HACKATHON EVENT DESIGNED TO CHALLENGE YOUR PROBLEM-SOLVING SKILLS AND CREATIVITY. COMPETE WITH THE BRIGHTEST MINDS, SOLVE REAL-WORLD PROBLEMS, AND SHOWCASE YOUR TALENT IN ALGORITHMS, WEB DEVELOPMENT, AND SOFTWARE ENGINEERING.

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS

  • TEAM-BASED CODING CHALLENGES
  • WORKSHOPS ON MODERN WEB TECHNOLOGIES
  • NETWORKING WITH INDUSTRY PROFESSIONALS
  • EXCITING PRIZES AND CERTIFICATES FOR WINNERS

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE?

THE HACKATHON IS OPEN TO ALL UNDERGRADUATE AND POSTGRADUATE STUDENTS PASSIONATE ABOUT CODING AND TECHNOLOGY. WHETHER YOU ARE A BEGINNER OR AN EXPERIENCED DEVELOPER, THERE IS A PLACE FOR YOU AT CODE OLYMPICS!

RECRUITMENT & OPPORTUNITIES

TOP PERFORMERS WILL GET A CHANCE TO BE RECRUITED FOR WEB DEVELOPMENT ROLES AND INTERNSHIPS. SHOWCASE YOUR SKILLS AND GRAB THE OPPORTUNITY TO WORK WITH LEADING TECH COMPANIES.
