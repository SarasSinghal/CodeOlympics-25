Hackathon Tracks

Explore the different challenges in Code Olympics

1.AI Innovation Challenge

Push the boundaries of artificial intelligence by creating solutions in areas like healthcare, education, and sustainability.

  • Duration: 24 hours
  • Team Size: 3–5 members
  • Judging Criteria: Creativity, Feasibility, Impact

2.Web Wizards Sprint

Build responsive, user-friendly websites or web apps that solve real-world problems and showcase creativity.

  • Duration: 12 hours
  • Team Size: 2–4 members
  • Judging Criteria: Design, Functionality, Usability

3.Cybersecurity Capture the Flag

A challenge designed to test your ethical hacking skills, where teams must solve puzzles and capture hidden “flags”.

  • Duration: 8 hours
  • Solo or Teams of 2
  • Judging Criteria: Number of flags captured

4.Game Development Jam

Design and build fun, interactive games using any framework or engine of your choice. Show off your creativity and storytelling through gameplay.

  • Duration: 24 hours
  • Team Size: 2–5 members
  • Judging Criteria: Creativity, Gameplay, Visual Appeal

5.Blockchain Builders

Create decentralized applications (dApps) or smart contracts that solve real-world problems using blockchain technology.

  • Duration: 18 hours
  • Team Size: 3–6 members
  • Judging Criteria: Innovation, Security, Practicality

6.IoT & Hardware Challenge

Integrate sensors, microcontrollers, or smart devices to build solutions that connect the digital and physical world.

  • Duration: 24 hours
  • Team Size: 3–5 members
  • Judging Criteria: Functionality, Hardware Integration, Impact

7.Open Innovation Hack

A freestyle hackathon where participants can work on any project idea of their choice, across any technology or domain.

  • Duration: 12 hours
  • Team Size: Flexible
  • Judging Criteria: Originality, Execution, Presentation
